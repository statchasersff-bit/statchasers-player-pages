4) Handling bye weeks / missing weeks

If the player didn’t have a game (bye / inactive), don’t include the week in the season average.

If team_total_targets = 0 (rare, but can happen with bad/missing data), skip that week.

5) Labeling (avoid confusion)

Because your denominator is pass-catcher targets, label it clearly in UI:

Target Share (of team pass-catcher targets)
or
Target Share (team targets)

That prevents “shouldn’t it be pass attempts?” comments.
