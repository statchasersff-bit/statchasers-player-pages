Section 1 — Career Snapshot (Top Card)

Layout: 1 card, left-aligned, 3–8 bullets max.

Content rules

1 bullet = 1 “meaningful career fact”

Use absolute dates / seasons (avoid “last year”)

If you don’t know something reliably, omit it

Suggested bullet types

Draft: year + round/pick + team (if available)

Early career: “entered as backup / immediate starter”

Breakout: first fantasy-elite season / role shift

Peak: best season / best fantasy finish (if you compute this)

Notable injuries: only if they materially affected games/role

Current role: 1 line summary (“locked-in starter / committee / WR1”)

Section 2 — Play Style → Fantasy Translation (Two Cards)

Layout: 2 cards in a row on desktop, stacked on mobile.

Card A: “How he wins”

Short paragraph + 2–4 tags.

Tags examples: “Dual-threat”, “Pocket passer”, “Red-zone threat”, “Target earner”, “YAC creator”

Card B: “Fantasy impact”

Short paragraph + 2–4 tags.

Tags examples: “Rushing floor”, “TD volatility”, “Volume-driven”, “Boom/Bust”, “High weekly ceiling”

Rule: no tables here. No percentiles. No metrics.

Section 3 — Career Timeline (Vertical Timeline)

Layout: vertical timeline with 4–8 entries.

Each entry

Season label (e.g., “2018”, “2020–2021”)

1 sentence describing the meaningful shift

Optional micro-badge: “Breakout”, “Injury”, “Role change”, “Peak”, “New team”

Auto-generation idea (optional)

Detect: team changes, first season above X PPG, missed games spikes, top finish season

Section 4 — “Fantasy Career Context” (Micro Summary Grid)

Layout: 4 mini tiles (not a stat table). These should be “meaningful” and not shown elsewhere.

Examples (choose 4):

Floor driver: “Rushing TD equity / target share / goal-line role”

Ceiling driver: “Explosive plays / deep targets / spike weeks”

Stability note: “Role consistency” (simple label like Stable / Moderate / Volatile)

Risk note: “Injury history / role competition / scheme volatility”

Each tile is 1–2 lines, not numbers.

Section 5 — Narrative Bio (Readable, Collapsible)

Layout: clean text block, max width ~70ch, with “Read more”.

Structure

Paragraph 1: origin + entry to league (college/draft/early role)

Paragraph 2: development + key turning point(s)

Paragraph 3: current role + what matters for fantasy managers

Length

Default visible: ~120–180 words

Expanded: ~350–600 words

Tone

Neutral, confident, fantasy-aware

No hype. No hot takes.

Section 6 — Sources + Last Updated (Trust Footer)

One thin footer row:

“Last updated: Feb 28, 2026”

Sources (2–5 links): Team site bio, NFL profile, PFR, etc.

This single line massively increases credibility.

UI/UX Spec (Premium look)

Sticky sub-nav within tab (optional): Snapshot · Style · Timeline · Bio

Cards: same blueprint theme as your other tools (navy border, subtle grid, gold underline on section headers)

Mobile-first: timeline collapses to 4 entries + “Show full timeline”

Empty-state logic: if missing timeline items, hide the section cleanly (don’t show placeholders)
