the Team Board should feel like a StatChasers module, not a separate “mode” with a totally different theme.

So: white-first, navy for structure, gold for premium accents, and position colors only as small utility accents (not the visual identity).

Here’s a StatChasers-native design system for the Team Board (layout + card styling) that will match your site instantly.

StatChasers Team Board Theme Rules
Color hierarchy (non-negotiable)

Background: White (#ffffff)

Structure / text: Navy (#0b1220 / #0f172a)

Accents: Gold gradient (#caa14a → #f4d27a)

Position colors: tiny elements only (pill / 3px border)

Premium feel comes from:

white space

soft shadows

rounded corners

subtle borders

gold used sparingly

✅ Layout that matches StatChasers
Page header (white, not dark)

Top bar (same spacing as your other tools):

← League View button (outline)

Team pill (logo + “BUF Bills”)

Right side: “Fantasy Roster Board” label + optional toggle (“Show Depth”)

Gold underline below header (thin, subtle).

✅ Player cards (StatChasers style)

Instead of full-bleed dark image cards, do a white card with an image block at top.

Card anatomy

Top: image area (light background, not dark)

Bottom: name area (white)

Micro-row: role tags (WR1, Starter, etc)

Position pill uses Sleeper color

Why this fits your brand

It matches your current tiles and pill design language, but upgrades it.
