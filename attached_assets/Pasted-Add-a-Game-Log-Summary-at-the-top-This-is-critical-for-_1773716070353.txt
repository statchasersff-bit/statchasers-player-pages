Josh Allen’s 2025 game log shows the profile of a high-end fantasy quarterback capable of producing elite weekly ceilings while maintaining a relatively stable scoring floor. Across 17 games, Allen averaged 22.0 fantasy points per game and delivered QB1 finishes in 65% of his appearances.

His best performance came in Week 11 against Tampa Bay, where he scored 44.7 fantasy points behind a multi-touchdown outing with rushing production. Even in quieter weeks, Allen’s rushing usage helped prevent complete fantasy busts, with no games falling into the QB37+ range.

The game log also reveals some late-season cooling, with fantasy production declining over the final stretch despite stable rushing involvement. Overall, Allen’s weekly results confirm his status as one of the most reliable high-ceiling fantasy quarterbacks.
