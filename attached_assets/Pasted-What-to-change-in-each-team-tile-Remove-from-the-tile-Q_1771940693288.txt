Team logo source (two clean options)
Option A: Use Sleeper team logos (easy + consistent)

You can map team abbreviations to Sleeper’s team IDs and use their logo CDN.

Option B: Store your own logos locally (most “pro” long-term)

Put SVG/PNG logos in your plugin:
/assets/logos/BUF.svg etc.

Then:

const logoUrl = `${scPlayersConfig.pluginUrl}assets/logos/${teamAbbr}.svg`;
